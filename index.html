I'm Giga Khizanishvili, a Senior iOS Engineer and Mobile Architect based in Tbilisi, Georgia (UTC+4). I studied Computer Science & Mathematics — which taught me that the best solutions are elegant, not complicated. That mindset drives everything I build.

Currently, I lead iOS architecture for 50+ engineers across 8 product teams at Space International (TBC UZ), a 3,000+ employee fintech organization. The Design System and infrastructure I built powers apps across 3 companies and is used by ~100 developers daily. I led a complete SwiftUI migration with zero production regressions, achieved 90%+ test coverage, and cut release cycles from 2 weeks to 3 days.

Beyond code, I spent 3 years as an Algorithms instructor, and I still believe the best way to master something is to explain it to others. I'm also an ACM-ICPC finalist who represented Georgia on the international stage.
